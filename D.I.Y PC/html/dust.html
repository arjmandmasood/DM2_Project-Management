Cleaning CPU Fan

A CPU fan can easily be cleaned using simple tachniques. It also does not require the PC to be taken apart, only opened using the side panel.

Here's what you will need:

-Cotton Buds

-Isopropyl Alchohol (Not necessary, although it can help the cleaning process)


Method:

Begin by carefully wiping any heavy layers of dust from the fan blades and the heatsink below.

Next, put some of the Isopropyl Alchohol on a cotton bud and wipe and hard to reach places to get rid of stuck dust.


Re-install the cleaned fan, and enjoy better airflow.
