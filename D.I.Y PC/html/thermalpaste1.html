Cleaning Old Thermal Paste

A heatsink and CPU chip can be cleaned easily. Once they are removed from the motherboard it only takes two steps to clean them. If you need help removing the fan, please refer to this page

Here's what you will need:

-Paper towel

-Thermal paste cleaner (can be purchased at any computer parts store)


Method:

Put a few drops of thermal paste remover onto a paper towel.

Wipe off the old thermal paste making sure the thoroughly clean every part.

Reapply thermal paste remover as necessary, making sure to never apply thermal paste remover directly to the parts


To re-apply the thermal paste, refer to the re-applying thermal paste page
